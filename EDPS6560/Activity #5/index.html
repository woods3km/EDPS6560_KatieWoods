About

My name is Katie Woods and I am a student in the Instructional Design and Educational Technology Masters Program at the University of Utah. I currenty live in Salt Lake City, UT but am originally from West Virginia.

My current job is as an assistant professor and clinical coordinator of education for our undergraduate athletic training program here at the U. I just completed my 5th complete year of teaching a variety of sports medicine related courses. Check out more about my courses in the "Education" section below.

When I am not working, I love to spend time with my boyfriend, and our two dogs Shiner and Kari. I love the outdoors, especially being on and around whitewater, mountain biking, and playing on snow.
